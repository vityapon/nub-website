About us

Esthetic Furniture started as a small workshop in Russia, Ekaterinburg, with a simple mission: to create furniture that combines timeless design with exceptional craftsmanship.

Founded by master carpenter James Wilson, our company has grown from a one-person operation to a team of 50 dedicated artisans, designers, and furniture enthusiasts.
