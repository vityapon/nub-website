FAQ

Q: Where is your furniture made?

A: Every piece is handcrafted in our Russian, Ekaterinburg workshop by a team of 12 master artisans. We believe in slow, intentional craftsmanship and complete supply chain transparency.

Q: What materials do you use?

A: We work exclusively with solid hardwoods (oak, walnut, maple), never particleboard or veneers. Our upholstery uses natural materials like linen, cotton, and leather from ethical sources. All finishes are water-based and non-toxic.

Q: Do you offer customization?

A: Yes! We offer custom dimensions, wood types, finishes, and fabric choices for most pieces. Custom orders require 8-12 weeks for production. Contact our design team at estheticfurniture@gmail.com to begin.
